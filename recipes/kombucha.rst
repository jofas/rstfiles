Kombucha (neat)
===============


Ingredients
-----------

* 1 (heaped) portion black tea

* 100g sugar

* 1l water

* a scoby obviously


Description
-----------

* make tea

* let it cool

* throw scoby in

* close batch with cloth (to keep flies away while still enabling
  oxygen circulation)

* wait at least 7 days


Notes
-----


TOTRY
-----

* less sugar

* plant-based syrups instead of processed sugar
